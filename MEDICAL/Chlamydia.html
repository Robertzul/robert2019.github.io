CHLAMYDIA

Chlamydia is an STD caused by Chlamydia Trachomatis (C.trachomatis).
This bacterium only infects humans.
Chlamydia is most common infectious cause of Genital and Eye Diseases Globally.
It is also the most common bacterial STD.

Women with Chlamydia do not usually show symptoms.
Any symptom are usually non-specific and may inlude:


1. Bladder infection
2. A change in vaginal discharge
3. Mild lower abdominal pain

If a person does not receive treatment for Chlamydia,
It may lead to the folowing Symptoms:


1. Pelvic pain
2. Painful sexual intercourse,either intermittently or every time
3. Bleeding between periods
