WHAT IS SPACE

First, some simple answers: space is everything in the Universe beyond the top of the Earth's atmospheres - the Moon, where GPS satellites orbit, Mars, other stars, the Milky Way, black holes, and distant quasars. Space also means what's between planets, moons, stars, erc - it's the near-vacuum otherwise known as the interplanetary medium, the interstellar medium, the inter-galactic medium, the intra-cluster medium, etc; in other words, it's very low density gas or plasma. (' space physics' is, in fact, just a branch of plasma physics!).
